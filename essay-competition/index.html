Saare Community Essay Competition

Empowering the youth through knowledge and creativity

About the Event

Join us in this exciting essay competition, where secondary school students in Saare community can showcase their writing skills and win amazing prizes!

Eligibility

This competition is open to all secondary school students in Saare community.

Entry Deadline

Essay submission begins September 2nd, 2024, and ends December 2nd, 2024.

Topic

The Role of Education in Enhancing Economic Opportunities in Saare

Guidelines

  • Font: Times New Roman, Font Size: 12
  • Length: Not more than 3000 words
  • Plagiarism: Plagiarized content will result in disqualification of the offender’s entry

Prizes

Winners will receive exciting prizes, which could be scholarships and other educational materials!

Mode of Submission

Submit the following documents in a single Word document along with your essay write-up:

  • A scan copy of school ID card
  • A scan copy of last school fees payment
  • A passport photograph
  • Student's phone number

Send your submission to: abdulconsole@gmail.com
